Travel

By Plane

Logro침o (RJL) has an airpot but it only has 1 connection, a direct flight with Madrid once a day. You can book it on Iberia. On the map you can see the nearby airports, the best option if you are coming from Europe is to try to get to Bilbao (BIO) since it quite some connections all over. Then you can take the train, bus, rent a car or share the ride with someone else (hint: there's the #travel channel on the official Slack of NSSpain!)
