Travel

By Plane

A direct flight connects Madrid with Logroño (RJL). You can book it at theIberia website

By Train

Several trains arrive to Logroño from the whole country. More information is available at the Renfe website.

By Bus

A four hours from Madrid or less than six hours from Barcelona trip, you could also arrive to Logroño by bus. Alsa is the largest bus company of Spain.

By Car

The easiest way is to plan your trip using Google Maps or Waze 😀

Accommodation

We work closely with a travel agency to help you on your trip to NSSpain. Drop a few lines to infologrono@pisamundo.com, Att. to Laura C. Del Cerro, and she will try to take care of everything you need.

Because of the wine festival (see next section below) it's usually all booked during the weekend, so if you're planning to stay a bit longer, wait no more and book right now!

San Mateo

Wine Festival

From September 15th to 22th there's San Mateo, our wine festival, the biggest festivity of the year in Logroño. Everybody could stay a little longer (during the weekend or more days), and enjoy with us one of the best wine festivities on earth! Here's a very good post about it!
