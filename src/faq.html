FAQ

Will all the content be in English?

Yes! We are an all-English conference, so everything will be in English: Talks, Workshops, Papers... Don't worry, wine and food are spanish.

Why 15th and 16th?

Because from 17th to 24th we celebrate San Mateo, our wine festival, the biggest festivity of the year in Logro√±o. Everybody can extend a little more the trip, during the weekend (or more days), and enjoy with us one of the best wine festivities on Earth! Here's a very good post about it!

What's the difference between the Early Bird tickets and the Standard one?

Both are the same, both give you the same access level, goodies, conditions, etc. the price is the only one difference. Early Bird tickets are for the first attendees who buy them, so that's why they're a little cheaper.

Do you have discounts for groups?

We do, if you're a group of 5 or more, drop us a line at info@nsspain.com

Do you have discounts for students?

We always try, if you're a student, drop us a line at info@nsspain.com

Can I get an invoice?

Absolutely. You can get the invoice directly from the Tito platform (where you bought the ticket). Check the email of the ticket, from there you can get directed into getting the invoice and change VAT and other details as needed.
